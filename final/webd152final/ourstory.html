The Story Behind Us

At Pacific Trails Resort, we ensure that your mind is set at ease. Our team is dedicated to providing our guests with exceptional experiences and creating a memorable vacation to every single guest at our stay.

While traveling around the globe, it all began when our founders stumbled upon an idea that would ultimately lead to the creation of our resort. As they journeyed through various destinations, they realized the transformative power of nature on the human experience. They wanted to find a way to share this with others and create a space where people could connect with the natural world and each other.

Our Values & Passions

As a growing team, Pacific Trails Resort aims to expand our operations, enhance our services, and ultimately prepare for the opening of our new resort at Yakama, Washington. We are committed to maintaining the natural beauty of the area and preserving the environment for future generations to enjoy.

This is a wonderful invitation to get a feel of the beauty and serenity of nature. The oceans, trees and sunlight are essential elements that provide us our joyfulness and prosperity. Breathing in the fresh ocean air and feeling the crispiness of air can be incredibly revitalizing.

Quality Experience
